We started Esoteric to find our special kind of people who have a deep love for books and reading. To invoke a sense of magic and transformation, suggesting that reading can be a transformative experience that opens up new possibilities and perspectives. It's the awakening of more mysterious and poetic readers who are looking for something a bit more esoteric.We encourage everyone anywhere to come to our store and be inspired to read and expand their knowledge and think critically and creatively.
